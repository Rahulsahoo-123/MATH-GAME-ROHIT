Algebra

  • (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
  • (a - b)² = a² - 2ab + b²
  • (a + b)(a - b) = a² - b²
  • (x + y + z)² = x² + y² + z² + 2xy + 2yz + 2zx
  • (a + b)³ = a³ + b³ + 3ab(a + b)
  • (a - b)³ = a³ - b³ - 3ab(a - b)
  • a³ + b³ = (a + b)(a² - ab + b²)
  • a³ - b³ = (a - b)(a² + ab + b²)
  • (x + y + z)³ = x³ + y³ + z³ + 3(x + y)(y + z)(z + x)
  • Sum of cubes: a³ + b³ + c³ - 3abc = (a + b + c)(a² + b² + c² - ab - bc - ca)
  • Quadratic formula: x = [-b ± √(b² - 4ac)] / 2a
  • Sum of roots: α + β = -b/a
  • Product of roots: αβ = c/a
  • Difference of squares: a² - b² = (a - b)(a + b)
  • Sum & difference of squares: a² + b² = (a + b)² - 2ab
  • Factorization shortcuts: a² + 2ab + b² = (a+b)²
  • Sum of n terms of arithmetic series: Sₙ = n/2 [2a + (n-1)d]

Arithmetic

  • nth term of AP: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
  • Sum of n terms of AP: Sₙ = n/2 [2a + (n-1)d]
  • nth term of GP: aₙ = a·rⁿ⁻¹
  • Sum of n terms of GP: Sₙ = a(1 - rⁿ)/(1 - r), r ≠ 1
  • Sum of first n natural numbers: Sₙ = n(n+1)/2
  • Sum of squares of first n natural numbers: Sₙ = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
  • Sum of cubes of first n natural numbers: Sₙ = [n(n+1)/2]²
  • HCF × LCM = Product of numbers
  • Arithmetic Mean (AM) = (Sum of terms)/n
  • Geometric Mean (GM) = √(Product of terms)
  • Harmonic Mean (HM) = n / Σ(1/xi)
  • Percentage change = [(New - Old)/Old] × 100
  • Ratio & Proportion: a:b = x:y → ax = by
  • Profit & Loss: Profit% = (Profit/CP)×100, Loss% = (Loss/CP)×100
  • Simple Interest = (P × R × T)/100
  • Compound Interest = P(1 + R/100)ⁿ - P

Geometry

  • Area of Triangle = ½ × base × height
  • Area of Rectangle = length × breadth
  • Area of Square = side²
  • Area of Parallelogram = base × height
  • Area of Trapezium = ½ × (sum of parallel sides) × height
  • Area of Circle = π × r²
  • Circumference of Circle = 2 × π × r
  • Diagonal of Square = side × √2
  • Diagonal of Rectangle = √(l² + b²)
  • Volume of Cube = a³
  • Surface Area of Cube = 6 × a²
  • Volume of Cuboid = l × b × h
  • Surface Area of Cuboid = 2(lb + bh + hl)
  • Volume of Cylinder = π × r² × h
  • Surface Area of Cylinder = 2πr(h + r)
  • Volume of Cone = 1/3 × π × r² × h
  • Surface Area of Cone = π × r(l + r)
  • Volume of Sphere = 4/3 × π × r³
  • Surface Area of Sphere = 4 × π × r²
  • Hemisphere: V = 2/3 × π × r³, SA = 3 × π × r²
  • Pythagoras Theorem: a² + b² = c²
  • Perimeter formulas: Rectangle = 2(l+b), Square = 4a, Circle = 2πr

Fractions

  • a/b + c/d = (ad + bc)/bd
  • a/b - c/d = (ad - bc)/bd
  • a/b × c/d = (ac)/(bd)
  • (a/b) ÷ (c/d) = (ad)/(bc)
  • Simplifying fractions: Divide numerator & denominator by GCD
  • Mixed number ↔ Improper fraction conversion
  • Comparing fractions: Cross-multiply or convert to decimals
  • Converting fraction to percentage: (a/b) × 100%
  • Converting percentage to fraction: (p/100) in simplest form

BODMAS Rules

  • B → Brackets ()
  • O → Orders (powers, roots)
  • D → Division ÷
  • M → Multiplication ×
  • A → Addition +
  • S → Subtraction -
  • Always solve left to right within same precedence
  • Nested brackets: Solve innermost first
  • Example: 5 + 3 × (2²) = 5 + 3 × 4 = 17

Percentage

  • Percentage = (Value / Total) × 100
  • Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100
  • Loss % = (Loss / CP) × 100
  • Simple Interest = (P × R × T)/100
  • Compound Interest = P(1 + R/100)ⁿ - P
  • Percentage Increase = [(New - Old)/Old] × 100
  • Percentage Decrease = [(Old - New)/Old] × 100
  • Fraction ↔ Percentage ↔ Decimal
  • GST / Tax: Final Price = Cost × (1 + Rate/100)
  • Discount % = (Discount / Marked Price) × 100
  • Successive discounts: Net factor = (1 - d₁/100) × (1 - d₂/100)
  • Successive increases: Net factor = (1 + r₁/100) × (1 + r₂/100)
